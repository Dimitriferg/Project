About

Kick'N IT is a pop-up sneaker store, specializing in the most popular sneaker brands. Kick'N IT has been around since 2016. Our store has held pop up shops in New York, NY Los Angeles, CA, Las Vegas, NV, and Atlanta, GA. We are now have the honor to host in the exciting city of Miami, FL.

Our Mission

Our mission is to unite sneakerheads all over the world with access to the most exclusive sneakers available.
